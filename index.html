Weekly Workout Reminder

Day 1: Chest & Triceps

  • Flat Bench Press - 4 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press - 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Chest Fly (Machine or Dumbbell) - 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Push-Ups - 3 sets to failure
  • Tricep Dips (Parallel Bars) - 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Overhead Tricep Extension (Dumbbell) - 3 sets x 12-15 reps

Day 2: Back & Biceps

  • Pull-Ups (Assisted if needed) - 4 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Lat Pulldown - 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Seated Row - 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Barbell Bicep Curl - 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Hammer Curls - 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Preacher Curl - 3 sets x 12-15 reps

Day 3: Shoulders & Abs

  • Overhead Press - 4 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Lateral Raises - 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Front Raises - 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Reverse Pec Deck - 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Shrugs - 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Plank Hold - 3 sets x 45-60 seconds

Day 4: Rest & Recovery

Take this day to rest and recover. Focus on stretching, foam rolling, and preparing for next week's workouts!

Day 5: Chest, Shoulders & Triceps

  • Incline Bench Press - 4 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Flat Dumbbell Fly - 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Arnold Press - 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Dumbbell Lateral Raises - 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Cable Tricep Pushdown - 3 sets x 12-15 reps

Day 6: Legs

  • Squats - 4 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Leg Press - 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Lunges - 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Leg Curls - 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Calf Raises - 4 sets x 15-20 reps

Day 7: Rest & Recovery

Take this day to rest and recover. Focus on stretching, foam rolling, and preparing for next week's workouts!
